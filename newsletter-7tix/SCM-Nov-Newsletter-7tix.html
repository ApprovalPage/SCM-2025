There are a lot of new additions to the market this year. One very exciting change is that you can now get tickets for as low as $7!
Here are a few other ways to save at the market this year:
Kids 6 and under - Enter for FREE.
Happy Hour - Enjoy food and beverage deals 4-6pm Monday through Thursday.
Buy ahead of time and online for the best deals.
Buy a pass or bundle that offers lower prices on tickets for the whole family and for transportation built in.
Bring a toy donation when you visit between 11/20-12/7 and receive a coupon to visit the market another time for FREE! Donations will go toward the KIRO 7 Cares Toy Drive, benefitting Toys for Tots and The Salvation Army.
No matter how/when you visit, your ticket includes snow showers, thousands of lights, walks through the wishing tree forest, elf scavenger hunts, carousel rides, live entertainment, photo opps, surprise market moments, and expedited shipping on all letters to Santa!
